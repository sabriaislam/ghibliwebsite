STUDIO GHIBLI

These are my absolute favorite Studio Ghibli movies. Enjoy!!

My Neighbor Totoro

The most iconic one is My Neighbor Totoro. I watched it with my buddies last year. Lots of good memories associated with it. And I always see tons of Totoro merch everywhere!!

Spirited Away

I loved the world-building in this one. All the spirits were so fun. My friend said I looked like the Stink spirit though :(

Grave of the Fireflies

Very different from other Ghibli movies. Tragic and heartbreaking. I cried for 2 hours after finishing it.
